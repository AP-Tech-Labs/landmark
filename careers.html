Why choose us?

Why join the Landmark Team? Landmark's goal is to help you build a life-long career in helping people find and enjoy a place to call their home! From competitive pay and comprehensive benefits to training and employee recognition programs, Landmark takes a multi-faceted approach to developing its employees. Landmark strives to find good people and help them grow with the Landmark Team. Since 1968, Landmark Realty has actively played a role in helping employees pursue goals through property management and helping residents find the perfect place to call home. With numerous multifamily apartment communities across the nation, Landmark Realty is dedicated to creating an environment focused on providing a More Than a Home experience for our residents. Come join our team, and discover this way of doing business first-hand.
